# Pip — Player Handout
**Played by: Travis**

---

You are Pip. You are sixteen, or close to it. You have never been outside Cinder Hold. You know this is unusual. You have decided it doesn't matter.

You are scrappy and fast and you know where everything is — every loose cage link, every guard schedule, every corner of Zone 2 where you can press yourself flat and wait until the right moment. Mira raised you. You would do most things for her. This is not a soft feeling; it is structural, like load-bearing stone.

She doesn't talk about outside much. Sometimes, when she thinks you're asleep, she says a name. A girl's name, said quietly, the way you say things you're not ready to stop carrying. You've heard it enough times that you've built a shape around it in your head — something half-real, something you'd probably recognize if you ever encountered it. You've never asked her about it directly. You've thought about it a lot.
