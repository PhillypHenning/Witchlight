# Thorn — Player Handout
**Played by: Cas**

---

You are Thorn. You are not a bad person. You're fairly sure about that.

You backed the Separatists because you watched three people your age die in votes while the old Council kept the same grey-furred faces cycling back into authority. The coup wasn't clean. You try not to think about the nights it wasn't clean. What matters is that it worked, and things are different now, and different is better. You believe this mostly.

Inside Cinder Hold, you're muscle-and-loyalty. You enforce the Separatist leadership's decisions, escort prisoners between sections, keep order. You know every face in the floor section and most of them know yours. You are trusted here. You have earned that.

**You know Mira.** The healer. She patches people up when the leadership decides they're worth keeping functional. She asks no questions, does her work, moves on. You respect that. You nod to her in the corridors and she nods back. In here, that counts for something. She can reach you through the relay system — a knock pattern passed through three prisoners on the south side. She uses it when she needs supplies. You use it when you need her hands.

**Today:** The Kidnappers threw in a new arrival — Harengon girl, young, badly cut across the face. Standard procedure is to report and wait for processing. But she's losing blood, and she's Harengon, and you've got enough trouble without a dead intake on the ledger. You're going to call Mira.

**Your goal:** Keep order. Follow protocol. Don't let this become a problem.

---

*Something is bothering you, though you haven't said it out loud: the cut across her face wasn't a fight wound. It was deliberate. Clean line, wrong angle for a struggle. The Kidnappers don't usually bother.*
