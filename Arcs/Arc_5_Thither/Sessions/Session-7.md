# Arc-5, Session-7

# Planning

## Recap

The morning after Sevro's funeral, you buried him properly and kept moving. Karag dreamed of three corridors and chose the hand of the starry figure in the dark. Di made friends with a donkey — named it Eddie, said naming it Sevro felt a little too soon — and tucked the bloodied crown into the saddlebag before getting any sleep.

North of the mushroom circle you found an old man in iron-jointed armor arguing with sentient fungi, and a small fairy cleric trying very hard to manage him. Jinx will not explain himself. Lindsay has been hired to help, though he insists that is not what is happening. They came with you north, which was fine until the Jabberwocky burst from the treeline and killed the owlbear Di had just healed, scattered Saren sixty feet into the undergrowth, and left carrying the corpse without apparent concern. Di collected the talons. The donkey watched without moving. Jinx attempted to mark Eddie for butchering and was stopped.

The trail led north until the trees stopped and the world turned to ash. On the horizon: the Cinderhold. A world-tree converted into iron. As tall as the canopy. It looked back at you. The session ended in the cinder dunes with animated armor bursting from the ground, the prison still distant, rocks being hurled from somewhere far above.

   a. Pick up mid-combat: 2 animated armors active, Pass Without Trace ~5 turns remaining
   b. Animated armors continue emerging each round; flying swords may reappear; Cinderhold hurls rocks periodically (DEX save DC 13, 2d10 bludgeoning)

2. **Retreat & Observation**
   a. As the party withdraws, a cloaked figure exits Cinderhold unimpeded — gate opens and closes without ceremony; figure walks south into the ash
   b. The cloak is canvas, dark grey; emblem visible on the back: a six-sided die with a skull on the one-facing side
   c. Perception DC 13 — the emblem matches the interior lining of the cloak recovered from the dead kidnapper
   d. Do not point this out if no one checks — the clue is there to be found, not delivered
   e. Party has one [Warden's Pass](../../../Worlds/General/Magic-Items/Wardens-Pass.md) — admits one person; they must plan around that

   c. The party may connect this to the forgotten names pattern; they may not; either is fine
