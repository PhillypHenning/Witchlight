# Beats
1. While the players are making their way through a particularly thick part of the forest rain begins to fall, escalating to pelting rain very quickly.
2. The players hear whimpering a short distance away from a bush
3. Investigating the bush, they find two children curled up and clutching each-other, both appear to be sobbing.
4. Speaking with them, they will warn the players of the orphanage telling them that the carekeeper is a monster who turns their few toys into other monsters and who eats children. They claim to have escaped a few nights ago and ran into forest and have been dodging kidnappers ever since. They don't remember where the orphanage is.
5. After sometime when the players take their eyes off them, they will disapear
