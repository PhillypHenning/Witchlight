# Cinder Hold Prison

## Overview
A prison complex where Skabatha Nightshade keeps prisoners, particularly Harengon (Yanaba's birth parents among them). The prison serves as both a holding facility and a source of "materials" for Skabatha's toymaking endeavors.

- Yanaba was swapped at birth by cruel pixies (Feylost background)

- Skabatha knows about Yanaba and wants to "complete the family"

## Escape Difficulty
- **High security**: Skabatha doesn't want her resources escaping
- **Magical wards**: Prevent simple teleportation solutions
- **Multiple guards**: Can't just fight through
- **Stealth or cleverness required**: Brute force likely to fail
- **Time pressure**: If Yanaba captured, Skabatha may move her quickly

## Complications
- **Alarm system**: Guards alerted quickly
- **Hostage situation**: Guards threaten other prisoners
- **Skabatha's attention**: She may come personally if there's a major incident
- **Pursuit**: Escaped prisoners are hunted
- **Guilt**: Can't save everyone, must leave some behind

## Loot & Rewards
- **Prisoners' gratitude**: Future allies in Thither
- **Information**: About Skabatha, the Toyhouse, and Thither
- **Key/Map**: To other Skabatha facilities
- **Skabatha's notes**: Revealing her plans
- **Magic items**: Confiscated from prisoners

## Aftermath
- **If successful rescue**: Made an enemy of Skabatha (if not already)
- **If Yanaba captured and not rescued**: Long-term quest, darker timeline
- **If prisoners freed**: Potential allies, but also refugees needing protection
- **Skabatha's response**: Increased security, hunting parties, retaliation

## Atmosphere & Themes
- **Despair**: Many prisoners have lost hope
- **Family**: Separation and longing to reunite
- **Identity**: Yanaba confronting her birth parents vs. adoptive family
- **Choice**: Who to save when you can't save everyone
- **Rebellion**: Some prisoners may fight back during rescue
