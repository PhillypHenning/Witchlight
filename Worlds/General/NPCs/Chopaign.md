## Overview
Chopaign is an archdevil who serves as patron to both Sevro and Di, though under vastly different circumstances. He is calculating, manipulative, and operates with the long-term scheming typical of infernal powers.

## The Casino Contract Situation

### Di's Mistake
Di made a contract with the Starved Goblin Casino owners (Velisara and Caldrien, both fiends) to provide **Chopaign's true eye color** within 2 months, or owe 100 starvens immediately.

**Critical oversight**: Di never asked who Velisara and Caldrien's boss is.

### When Di Asks About Eye Color

**Chopaign catches on immediately** to why Di is asking.

**His Response:**
1. **Scolds Di** for making deals with devils without learning who they serve
   - "You made a deal with devils and didn't learn who they serve? Foolish."
   - Makes it clear this is a basic failure in dealing with infernal powers
   
2. **Compares power levels** (subtly)
   - Mentions that the casino owners' boss's power is comparable to his own
   - Implies this is significant, but doesn't make it obvious how powerful he actually is
   - The comparison is designed to make Di realize he's dealing with major players
   
3. **Issues a counter-deal** (carrot and stick)
   - **What Chopaign wants**: Information about the infernal hierarchy
   - **Options for Di**:
     - Learn the casino owners' **boss's name**
     - OR learn the owners' **true names** (Velisara and Caldrien's real infernal names)
   - **Only then** will Chopaign provide his eye color to fulfill Di's contract
   
4. **The lesson**: Information is currency in the Nine Hells
   - True names grant power over devils
   - Understanding hierarchies is essential for survival
   - Di traded for something he can't deliver without doing more work

## The Soul Exchange Plan

**Hidden from Sevro and Di:**
Chopaign has a cruel plan centered on tormenting Sevro, but neither Sevro nor Di are aware of it yet.

**The Plan:**
1. **Sevro's father's soul** is held by a rival of Chopaign's (identity TBD)
2. Send **Di and the party** to retrieve the father's soul from this rival
3. Provide each party member with a **Scroll of Return** to bring them back to Chopaign's sitting room (where he usually converses with Di)
4. Once Chopaign has the father's soul, **offer Sevro a choice**:
   - Continue with his current fate (1000 years with Mr. Hat, eternity with Chopaign)
   - OR replace himself with his father (father suffers the torment instead)
5. **Either way, Chopaign wins**: The strife and grief he causes Sevro is what he's truly after

**The Cruelty:**
- Sevro must choose between his own suffering or damning his father
- Even if Sevro sacrifices himself, Chopaign gets to watch him agonize over the choice
- The emotional torment is the real prize for the archdevil
- Di will have unknowingly helped orchestrate Sevro's torture
