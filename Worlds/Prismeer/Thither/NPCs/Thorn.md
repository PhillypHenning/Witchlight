# Thorn

**Role**: Separatist enforcer, Cinder Hold Prison
**Faction**: Separatists (muscle-and-loyalty)
**Status**: Active — controls intake, keeps order in general population

A young, strong Harengon who backed the coup because he watched his generation die in "fair" votes while the elders survived. He is not cruel — he is tired, angry, and convinced he is finally on the right side of something. He enforces Separatist decisions, manages new arrivals, and keeps the ward from falling apart. He has more doubt than he shows.

---

## Stat Block

**Thorn**
*Medium humanoid (Harengon), neutral*

**Armor Class** 13 (leather armor)
**Hit Points** 32 (5d8 + 10)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 15 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 11 (+0) | 9 (−1) |

**Skills** Athletics +4, Perception +4
**Senses** passive Perception 14
**Languages** Common, Sylvan
**Challenge** 1/2 (100 XP)

**Hare-Trigger.** Thorn adds his proficiency bonus to initiative rolls.

**Lucky Footwork.** When Thorn fails a Dexterity saving throw, he can use his reaction to add 1d4 to the result.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Thorn makes two Shiv attacks.

**Shiv.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

---

## Roleplaying Notes
- Speaks plainly; doesn't dress things up
- Will not harm a prisoner without cause — but his definition of "cause" is broad
- If shown evidence of the rigged voting, he goes quiet for a long moment before responding
- Can be reasoned with; cannot be charmed or bullied
