# Hank Hill - The Wandering Merchant of Coal and Culinary Delights

## Basic Information

**Race:** Human
**Class:** Commoner (with exceptional cooking and survival skills)
**Background:** Guild Artisan (Specialty: Culinary and Trade)

---

## Appearance

Hank Hill is a middle-aged human with a sturdy yet unassuming build. He sports short-cropped, brownish hair with a receding hairline and wears a pair of simple but rugged glasses. He is often dressed in practical, sturdy clothes suitable for traveling and manual labor, always with an apron ready at hand. His expression is typically one of calm determination and sincere politeness.

---

## Personality

- **Honest**: Hank's no-nonsense attitude and unwavering principles make him a trustworthy figure
- **Hardworking**: Believes strongly in the value of good, honest labor
- **Stubborn**: Can be somewhat rigid in his ways, particularly about proper cooking methods
- **Kind-hearted**: Always willing to lend a hand to those in need
- **Passionate**: His love for grilling and cooking is second only to his passion for trading quality coal

---

## Backstory

Hank Hill hails from a hard-working family of artisans in a small village. Raised with a strong work ethic and a passion for quality, Hank took up the family trade but found his true calling in the synergy of coal and cooking. His wanderlust was ignited by tales of distant markets and exotic ingredients, driving him to become a wandering cook and trader. Now, he travels the lands, offering premier coal and culinary wisdom to all who cross his path.

---

## Goals

- Find new markets for his high-quality coal throughout Hither
- Perfect the art of coal-fired cooking in exotic locations
- Share the gospel of proper grilling techniques with the denizens of the Feywild
- Make an honest living through trade and culinary services

---

## Skills and Abilities

- **Expertise in Survival and Cooking**: Specializes in foraging, preparing meals, and grilling
- **Merchant's Eye**: Can appraise goods and negotiate better prices due to his background
- **Artisan's Tools - Cook's Utensils**: Exceptionally skilled in preparing meals, particularly those requiring perfect coal usage

---

## Equipment

- **Coal Satchel**: A durable satchel filled with high-quality coal
- **Cook's Utensils**: A set of well-maintained cooking tools
- **Grill of the Flame Serpent**: A magical portable grill that allows him to cook anywhere
- **Merchant's Cart**: His trusty cart to carry various coal accessories and culinary supplies

---

## Tactics

- Hank will not participate in combat unless absolutely necessary
- If combat begins, his two primary goals are:
  1. Sell coal, coal accessories, or meals to those in combat
  2. Take his cart and run away
- If combat is unavoidable, he will use his fists to attack non-lethally before attempting to flee

---
