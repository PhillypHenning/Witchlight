# Yarnspinner's Spiders

Creatures that serve the ancient story-weaver Yarnspinner in the eastern forests of Thither. All font-type spiders share the Giant Spider as their base stat block, modified by one unique feature. The two elites — Helvetica and Futura — are distinct creatures with substantially upgraded statistics.

---

## Font Squad

*Seven types. Individually weak. Dangerous as a coordinated unit.*

All font spiders share the following base unless noted otherwise:

**Base — Font Spider**
*Medium Beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 14 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 26 (4d8 + 4)
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 14 (+2) | 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 2 (−4) | 11 (+0) | 4 (−3) |

**Skills** Stealth +7
**Senses** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
**Challenge** 1 (200 XP)

**Spider Climb.** The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without an ability check.

**Web Sense.** While in contact with a web, the spider knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.

**Web Walker.** The spider ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.

### Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 9 (2d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half on a success. On a failed save, the target is also poisoned until it makes the save at the end of one of its turns.

**Web (Recharge 5–6).** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target is restrained by webbing. As an action, the restrained target can make a DC 12 Strength check, bursting the webbing on a success.

---

### Arial

*Small and nondescript, with the standard eight-legged spider form. You've seen dozens like it.*

**Unique Feature — Knockoff.**
When Arial's Bite causes the poisoned condition on a target, a spider-copy of that target appears in an adjacent unoccupied space at the start of Arial's next turn. The copy has 10 HP, AC 12, speed 30 ft., and on each of its turns makes one Clumsy Strike (+3 to hit, reach 5 ft., 1d4 bludgeoning) against the original target. The copy disappears after 1 minute or when reduced to 0 HP. Only one copy can exist at a time.

*Arial is a well-known knockoff of Helvetica. The joke writes itself.*

---

### Impact

*Blocky and muscular, with a thick body and stumpy legs. Moves side to side like a crab.*

**Unique Feature — Bold.**
When Impact hits with its Bite, the target must make a DC 13 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the target is knocked back 10 feet and knocked prone.

---

### Comic Sans

*Lumpy and off-balance, with one leg longer than the others. Its body lists slightly to one side.*

**Unique Feature — Erratic.**
At the start of each of Comic Sans's turns, roll a d4 to determine its behavior:

| d4 | Behavior |
|----|----------|
| 1 | Attacks the nearest creature regardless of allegiance |
| 2 | Moves 20 ft. in a random direction and takes no other action |
| 3 | Acts normally |
| 4 | Shrieks — all creatures within 10 ft. must make a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw or have disadvantage on their next attack roll |

---

### Courier

*Lean and weathered, with faded color and worn leg joints. Its movements are mechanical and precise.*

**Unique Feature — Relentless.**
At the start of combat, Courier marks one target it can see. Courier has advantage on all attack rolls against its marked target. When the marked target moves, Courier can use its reaction to move up to its speed toward that target.

---

### Garamond

*Slender and graceful, with long tapering legs and a body that seems to taper toward the rear. Iridescent in certain light.*

**Unique Feature — Precise.**
Garamond scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. It ignores half and three-quarters cover. On a critical hit, the poisoned target cannot make a Constitution saving throw to end the poisoned condition until the start of Garamond's next turn.

---

### Wingdings

*Covered entirely in sharp, angular markings and dark protrusions. Its shape seems to shift depending on how you look at it.*

**Unique Feature — Illegible.**
When Wingdings hits with its Bite, in addition to normal damage, roll a d6:

| d6 | Additional Effect (until end of target's next turn) |
|----|------|
| 1 | Blinded |
| 2 | Silenced |
| 3 | Frightened of Wingdings |
| 4 | Poisoned (stacks with bite poison) |
| 5 | Charmed by Wingdings |
| 6 | No additional effect |

---

### Papyrus

*Elongated and tall, with legs that stand higher than the others. Its body is decorated with natural flourishes and rough texturing.*

**Unique Feature — Grand Entrance.**
Once per combat, when Papyrus first takes a turn, all creatures within 30 ft. must make a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of Papyrus until the end of their next turn. Papyrus is immune to this effect.

---

## Elites

*Two named lieutenants. Significantly more dangerous than the font squad.*

---

### Helvetica

*Large and well-proportioned, with clean lines and balanced features. Its body is nearly symmetrical.*

*Large Beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 75 (10d10 + 20)
**Speed** 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 18 (+4) | 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 4 (−3) | 12 (+1) | 6 (−2) |

**Skills** Stealth +5, Perception +4
**Senses** Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 60 ft., Passive Perception 14
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)

**Spider Climb.** As base.
**Web Sense.** As base.
**Web Walker.** As base.

**Universal (Passive).** All friendly spiders within 30 ft. of Helvetica gain +2 to attack rolls.

### Actions

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half on a success. On a failed save, the target is also poisoned.

**Web (Recharge 5–6).** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target is restrained by webbing (escape DC 14 Strength).

### Bonus Action

**Direct.** Helvetica grants one adjacent friendly spider an additional attack this turn.

---

### Futura

*Compact and angular, with sharply-angled legs and a streamlined body. Built for speed and precision movements.*

*Large Beast, unaligned*

**Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)
**Hit Points** 65 (10d10 + 10)
**Speed** 40 ft., climb 40 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 16 (+3) | 20 (+5) | 12 (+1) | 6 (−2) | 16 (+3) | 8 (−1) |

**Skills** Stealth +8, Perception +6
**Senses** Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., Tremorsense 60 ft., Passive Perception 16
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)

**Spider Climb.** As base.
**Web Sense.** As base.
**Web Walker.** As base.

*Futura takes no actions or bonus actions. It acts entirely through its reactions.*

### Reactions (3 per Round)

**Counterstrike (Melee).** *Trigger:* A creature within 5 ft. of Futura attacks a friendly spider.
*Response:* Futura makes one Bite attack against the triggering creature. *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 12 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage, and the target makes a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 9 (2d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half on a success. On a failed save, the target is poisoned.

**Preemptive Web (Ranged).** *Trigger:* A creature moves to within 60 ft. of Futura.
*Response:* Futura launches a web at the triggering creature. *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, range 60 ft. *Hit:* The target is restrained by webbing (escape DC 13 Strength).

**Venom Fog (Debuff).** *Trigger:* A creature Futura can see makes an attack roll or casts a spell.
*Response:* Futura spits a concentrated spray of venom (range 30 ft., no attack roll required). The triggering creature must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it has disadvantage on the triggering roll and is poisoned until the end of its next turn.

## Tactics Notes

- **Font squad** is meant to be individually manageable but collectively exhausting — stack types for compound effects (e.g. Courier marks a target, Garamond crits them, Wingdings debuffs them)
- **Helvetica** stays back and directs; killing it breaks the +2 bonus and stops the bonus action attacks
- **Futura** doesn't initiate — position it where it can punish aggression; players who spread out and move carefully will find it less threatening than those who rush
- **Comic Sans** is deliberately unpredictable — roll its behavior openly at the table for comedic effect
- **Arial's copies** should use the player's name when announced ("A blurred copy of Karag appears—")
